Lo Rays are Glasgow based, but thier members hail from Finland, England
and Scotland. Thier sound, other than, EPIC is so unique, made up of,glitchy synths, pop hooks and metal riffs.


In 2023 Lo Rays released a self-produced concept EP Algorithm Gods,   
  set in a post-apocalyptic dystopia, where a tech corporation CONNECTICA controls the masses. The songs link together to tell a story of a conditioned mind that wakes up and rises against the system.

Finnish songwriter Laura Takala formed Lo Rays in 2018 after moving to Glasgow following her dream to make music in the footsteps of her heroes  Biffy Clyro . She met Scott Bathgate(guitar), Ryan Jones (bass) and Murray Bartle (drums) at a local gig.


 On May 7, 2024 Lo Rays released self-produced single, Forget Myself. Forget Myself not only gained over 1000 plays in its 1st week of release, it has garnered considerable attention on BBC's Radio 1, where it is getting some airtime. The DJs at BBC's Radio 1 have expressed their admiration for the band and thier music.


Lo Rays kicked off thier Algorithm Gods Tour  Glasgow on 5th April to a SOLD OUT show at MC Chuills. It ended with another packed out 1st time show in London's Trinity Bar on 27th April!
