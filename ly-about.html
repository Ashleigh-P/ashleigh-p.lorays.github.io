Meet The Band

Laura
Scott
Ryan
Murray

Lo Rays formed in 2018 when singer-songwriter Laura Takala followed her dreams and moved from Finland to Glasgow to start a band and make music, inspired by her idols Biffy Clyro. Along the way, she met Ryan Jones (Bass), Scott Bathgate (Guitar) and Murray Bartle, (Drums).
Together they have been creating a unique visual and auditory experience for fans with a genuinely one of a kind sound that blends glitchy synths, pop hooks, and metal riffs, Lo Rays ticks all the boxes of a captivating live band. Their massive cyber anthems are sure to stay in your head for hours!
Each show features stunning homemade visuals created by Laura herself, delivered on unconventional retro Orion TVs. You can also check out the band's YouTube channel to see the incredible full music videos she creates. The band takes pride in making all their own props and outfits for each video, showcasing their incredible creativity. Scott Bathgate, a producer in his own right, also mixes and produces the music for band (and several others)
Recently, Lo Rays supported VUKOVI at Cathouse and completed their Algorithm Gods tour, which kicked off with a sold-out show at McChuills in Glasgow on 5th April and rounded off with another packed show in London's Trinity Bar on 27th April!
Currently, the band is focused on writing, recording, and shooting new material.
